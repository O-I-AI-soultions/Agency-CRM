# O-I

A two-person AI-powered business: a **web agency** for small businesses in Israel, fed by an **automated lead pipeline** that finds businesses with no website.

## The two tracks

| Track | What it does | Status |
|---|---|---|
| [Lead pipeline](pipeline/README.md) | Apify scrapes Google Maps → Make.com filters & routes → Airtable CRM. Finds businesses with a phone number but no website. | 🔨 In progress (current focus) |
| [Web agency](templates/README.md) | Template-based websites built with Claude Code, delivered in hours. Monthly maintenance plan for recurring revenue. | 📋 Planned |

## Why it works

The pipeline produces a list of local businesses that need a website. The agency sells them exactly that — faster and cheaper than freelancers, thanks to reusable templates + Claude Code.
